Triveni Ghat, Rishikesh

Triveni Ghat is the largest and most revered bathing ghat in Rishikesh, located on the banks of the holy Ganges River. It holds great religious significance as it is believed to be the confluence of three sacred riversâ€”Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati. Pilgrims from all over the country visit this ghat to take a holy dip in its waters, which is said to cleanse sins and grant salvation.

Every evening, Triveni Ghat comes alive with the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti, a ritualistic offering to the river performed with chanting, lamps, and devotional songs. The sight of hundreds of floating diyas in the river creates a divine atmosphere. Apart from its spiritual importance, Triveni Ghat is also a serene place where visitors can sit and meditate, soaking in the peaceful vibes of Rishikesh.
