Triveni Ghat, Rishikesh: A Sacred Confluence

Triveni Ghat is the largest and most revered bathing ghat in Rishikesh, located on the banks of the holy Ganges River. It holds great religious significance as it is believed to be the confluence of three sacred rivers—Ganga, Yamuna, and Saraswati. Pilgrims from all over the country visit this ghat to take a holy dip in its waters, which is said to cleanse sins and grant salvation.

Every evening, Triveni Ghat comes alive with the mesmerizing Ganga Aarti, a ritualistic offering to the river performed with chanting, lamps, and devotional songs. The sight of hundreds of floating diyas in the river creates a divine atmosphere. Apart from its spiritual importance, Triveni Ghat is also a serene place where visitors can sit and meditate, soaking in the peaceful vibes of Rishikesh.

Main Attractions at Triveni Ghat

1] Ganga Aarti (Maha Aarti)

The Maha Aarti at Triveni Ghat is the highlight of the evening. It is performed with oil lamps, chanting of Vedic hymns, and devotional songs. Hundreds of people gather to witness this mesmerizing ceremony, which is dedicated to Goddess Ganga. The aarti usually takes place around sunset, filling the air with divine energy.

2] Rishi Kund

A sacred pond near Triveni Ghat, believed to have been blessed by the Ganges.

3] Statue of Lord Shiva

A towering statue of Lord Shiva, partially submerged in the river, enhances the spiritual ambiance of the ghat.

4] Floating Diyas

Devotees release small oil lamps (diyas) into the Ganga, making a wish. The sight of hundreds of flickering lamps floating on the river is breathtaking.

Best Time to Visit

  • The best time to visit Triveni Ghat is early morning or during the evening aarti (around 6:00 PM – 7:00 PM).
  • The winter months (October to March) are ideal for a pleasant experience, while the summer months can be hot.

How to Reach Triveni Ghat

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun (about 20 km away).
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Rishikesh Railway Station (approximately 2 km away).
  • By Road: Rishikesh is well connected by road to major cities like Haridwar (25 km) and Dehradun (45 km). Local rickshaws, taxis, and buses are available.
